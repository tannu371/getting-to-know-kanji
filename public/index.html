Free sample — Chapter 1

Kanji: 日 (day / sun)

Meaning: day, sun. Stroke order and reading: にち / ひ

  1. Mnemonic: Imagine the sun as a simple square with a line — it lights the day.
  2. Vocabulary: 日本 (にほん) — Japan; 今日 (きょう) — today; 日曜日 (にちようび) — Sunday

Practice: Write the kanji 10 times, then form two sentences using 日本 and 今日.
